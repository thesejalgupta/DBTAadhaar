Why DBT and Aadhaar Verification?

What is DBT?

DBT is a system where government subsidies are directly transferred to the bank accounts of beneficiaries, reducing leakage and corruption.

The Need for Verification

Aadhaar linking ensures that the funds reach the intended person, eliminating fake or duplicate beneficiaries and ensuring transparency.

Tackling Fraud

By using Aadhaar, the system can cross-check beneficiary data across various schemes, significantly reducing the scope for financial fraud.

Ensuring Scholarship Access

A high percentage of students miss out on scholarships due to unseeded or incorrect bank account details. Aadhaar linking prevents this.
